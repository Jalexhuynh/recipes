Ingredients:
 - 1 Lechera Can
 - 4oz. Cream Cheese
 - 9oz. Pizza Crust
 - 3 Eggs

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. 
 
 2. Blend ingredients in blender until fully blended and smooth.

 3. Pour the mix into the premade pie crust.

 4. Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 35 minutes, or until fully brown.
